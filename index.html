How To Play

This game is modeled after a classic memory match game with a fighting game twist. It's been developed for 2 players.

Each player starts with 100 health and 15 attack power.

Attack power increases depending on how many matches are made in a row.

Inflict damage by flipping over two cards and matching them each turn.

Hint: Match the Brutality and Fatality cards to unlock game secrets
